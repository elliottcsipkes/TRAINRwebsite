What is TRAINR?

TRAINR is a web and app platform that allows personal trainers to connect with clients and manage their business more efficiently and effectively while also being a hub for consumers by providing workout plans, meal plans, and the ability to communicate with their trainer.

Built with the goal of eradicating preventable disease, the TRAINR platform serves as a tool to help trainers change their clients' lives.

Our mission is to build a platform that equips both personal trainers and ordinary people with the tools they need to build healthy lives.

Elliott Csipkes, Founder & CEO
